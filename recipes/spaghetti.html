Spaghetti and Meatballs

This recipe features boiled spaghetti noodles, delicious marinara sauce and a topping of parmesean cheese and italian meatballs. It's quick, easy, and delicious! Make sure to focus on cooking the meatballs to perfection and sprinkling a modest amount of parmesean cheese when serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 ounce of noodles
  • 1 cup of parmesean cheese
  • 3 ounces of spaghetti noodles
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp of dried parsley

  2. Boil Spaghetti noodles for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Empty water from pot and serve spaghetti with the sauce and meatballs on top!
